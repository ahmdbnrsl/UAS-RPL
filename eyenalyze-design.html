📌 Overview
A minimal but complete implementation of LeNet-5 trained on the MNIST handwritten digit dataset. Serves as a clean baseline for CNN experiments on this platform.
🏗️ Architecture
LeNet-5 with two conv layers, two pooling layers, and three fully connected layers. Total params: ~61K.
Conv2d(1→6, 5×5) → MaxPool
Conv2d(6→16, 5×5) → MaxPool
Flatten → FC(400→120→84→10)
Softmax output
🚀 Training
Trained for 10 epochs using SGD with momentum 0.9, learning rate 0.001, batch size 64.
Dataset: MNIST (60K train / 10K test)
Optimizer: SGD(lr=0.001, momentum=0.9)
Loss: CrossEntropyLoss
Final test accuracy: 99.2%
